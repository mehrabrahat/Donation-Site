Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about HopeFund and your donation.

Your Questions, Answered

Is my payment secure?

Yes. All donations are processed via SSLCOMMERZ, a secure and trusted payment gateway supporting cards and mobile wallets.

Can I donate from outside Bangladesh?

Depending on your card issuer and SSLCOMMERZ settings, many international cards are supported. You can also contact us if you face any issues.

How do I know where my money goes?

We work with partner organizations and share regular updates, stories, and reports so you can see the impact of your giving.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

This depends on your country’s laws and our registration status in your region. Please contact us for more information for your specific case.

Can I cancel or change a monthly donation?

Yes. If you set up a recurring monthly donation, you can contact us anytime to pause, change, or stop it.

Still have questions? Reach out to us — we are happy to help.
