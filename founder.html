Founder & CEO

Meet the woman whose compassion and vision inspired the creation of Bukky Ebun Charitable Foundation.

Bukky Ebun

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Bukky Ebun is a passionate humanitarian and advocate for vulnerable children. Her journey began with a deep desire to provide love, stability, and opportunity to children who have lost their mothers and lack access to basic care.

Through years of community involvement and personal sacrifice, Bukky witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by motherless children — from lack of education and nutrition to emotional neglect. This realization led to the establishment of the Bukky Ebun Charitable Foundation.

Today, her mission is to create a safe, nurturing environment where every child feels valued, supported, and empowered to reach their full potential.

“Every child deserves love, care, and a chance to dream — no matter their circumstances.”
